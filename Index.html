[info]

You gain time every second.

For every extra level of the time modulator you gain an additional 1 time/s.

For every temporal reset you get +0.2 time/s per level of time modulator.

The goal is to upgrade the effect of temporal resets so you gain more per level of the time modulator and thus more time/s.

α adds 1 per α to the amount of temporal resets you get per temporal reset, the key is that this affects the price of the next temporal reset the same as gaining 1 even tho you get more temporal resets.
