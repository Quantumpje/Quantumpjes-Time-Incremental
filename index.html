[info]

You gain time every second.

For every extra level of the time modulator you gain an additional 1 time/s.

For every temporal reset you get +0.5 time/s per level of time modulator.

The goal is to upgrade the effect of temporal resets so you gain more per level of the time modulator and thus more time/s.

α unlocks eating food, food gives you time based on the food and your taste of food, for each food you eat you gain taste.

β will unlock something in the future, it does nothing for now.

This game is still very WIP, dont expect a fully playable game!
